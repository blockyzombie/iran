Clothing

Many of Iran's people, including most city dwellers, wear clothing similar to the clothes worn by North Americans and Europeans.

Many women also wear long, usually black, body robes called chadors over their clothes. A woman wears a chador around her body, across her shoulders, and over her head. Sometimes, she also covers the lower part of her face. Wearing of a chador is a tradition based on Islamic teachings concerning modesty. The government requires women to wear chadors or other head coverings.
