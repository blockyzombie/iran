Education

The Islamic government strongly influences the Iranian way of life. It restricts freedom of speech and other civil rights. It bans all forms of entertainment that it considers to be un-Islamic. The government also requires schools to stress the teachings of Islam. In addition, the government subjects Iranian women to a strict dresscode and public behavior.
